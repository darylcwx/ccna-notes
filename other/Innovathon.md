## Business Cases

### AI Quotation Engine

- Sponsor: Pranay, HOD: Lawrence
- Cost estimation and Quote generation for standard network projects to AI agents
- Free up SS and SA resources to focus on high-value activities
- **Current Process**
	- There is a fixed format
	- **Manual process client email**
		- Extract requirements
		- Clarifying questions
	- **Manual cost preparation**
		- Requirement => BOM, services, maintenance, vendor pricing request, extracting SKU and discount from vendor quote
	- **Manually release quote**
		- Translate cost sheet into quotes
- **Key Benefits**
	- Pre-Sales and SS Focus on Strategic Accounts
	- Value to top clients
	- Early visibility
	- Increase coverage of customers and opportunities
	- Faster response = higher win rate
	- Enforce common standards in technical sizing and pricing
- **MVP**
	- Human check before sending the processed automated reply

### AI-enhanced Contract Renewal Automated Systems (ACRAS)

- Sponsor: Pranay, HOD: Lalit
- Contract Renewal process is manual from start to end
- **Challenges**
	- Ad-hoc renewal triggering (missed opportunities)
	- Fragile process integration (email is used)
	- Manual reconciliation of the large CIs (config items) (errors in quotation and commitments)
	- Time-to-respond (slow)
	- Inconsistent, inaccurate, delays
- **Solution**
	- Order booking system (SAP) > Quotation system (DIRECT) > Workflow tool > Financial Operations
	- New 'target operating model' on top of existing backend apps
	- Workflow backbone using Power Automate/aXet flows
- **Outcome**
	- Faster turnaround time for quality client proposals
	- Gain market share in the SG Managed Services business
	- Revenue growth
