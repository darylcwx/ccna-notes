## 1. Wi-Fi 7

- Increased use-cases but bandwidth hungry
- 6 GHz Wi-Fi 6E is mature
- Certification just got available
- Slight benefits to enterprise
- Security upgrade (client interop)
- Plan for >30W, plan for >= 1Gbps

### 2. Unified Licenses

- **Unified Licensing Model**: Combines hardware, software, and support into a single subscription.
- **Global Use Access Points**: Simplifies SKU management for global deployments.
- **Flexible Management**: Supports both on-prem and cloud-managed modes (Catalyst Center & Meraki).
- **Streamlined Onboarding**: Intelligent discovery of management mode reduces setup complexity.
- **Lifecycle Simplification**: Easier procurement, renewals, and upgrades via Cisco Networking Subscription.
- **Future-Proofing**: Designed to scale with Wi-Fi 7 and evolving enterprise needs.
